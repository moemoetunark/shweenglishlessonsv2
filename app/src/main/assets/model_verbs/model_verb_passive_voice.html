Passive: An exam may be given by our English teacher today.

Active : Juan can give them some information about the job.
အလုပ္နဲ႕ပတ္သက္လို႕ဂ်ဴအန္ကသူတို႕ကိုအခ်က္လက္နည္းနည္းေပးႏိုင္တယ္

Passive: Some information can be given about the job by Juan.
အလုပ္နဲ႕ပတ္သက္လို႕ဂ်ဴအန္ကသူတို႕ကိုအခ်က္လက္နည္းနည္းေပးႏိုင္တယ္

Active : You must obey the traffic rules.

လမ္းစည္းကမ္းကိုလိုက္နာရမယ္

Active: We must follow the rules.

Active: The government should ban smoking in the public.

Passive: Smoking in the public should be banned by the government.
