ကၽြန္ေနာ္သည္ အိမ္စာကိုညေနခင္းလုပ္လိမ့္မယ္
အနာဂတ္ဆက္လက္ (Future Continuous) I will be watching the football match on TV this evening.

အနာဂတ္ျပီးစီးဆက္လက္ (Future Perfect Continuous) I will have been doing my homework tonight.
ကၽြန္ေနာ္သည္အိမ္စာကိုဒီညလုပ္ျပီးေနလိမ့္မယ္		 My homework will have been being done tonight.
